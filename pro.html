XDAO Pro

XDAO Pro is a consulting service for creating unique DAOs exlusively to client's desires. We help to work out all the specifics of the organization, tokenomics, and the development of additional one-of-a-kind modules.

Problem

Want to create a DAO and don't know how to start? Probably you have a specific request for your DAO but building it on your own can take a long time to research and develop, even with existing DAO builders.

Solution

With XDAO Pro, you will get the product by contributing your time only to consultation. We provide legal and technical support throughout the creating process and after the launch. Count upon experts.

How to get started?

Fill out our form before booking an advisory, or let our manager help you by filling it right on the consultation.

Get ready to answer our questions about your DAO. We may need more than one consultation to provide you with the best result.
