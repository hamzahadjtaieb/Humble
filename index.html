How to get started?

STAKING :
CLAPs are staked to obtain the governance token. The holder the number of tokens that he wishes to allocate to this staking.

VOTE FOR A PROPOSITION :
Token holders vote for a proposal submitted by the teams Cardashift. They enter the number of tokens they want to allocate to their vote.

CREATE A VOTE PROPOSITION :
The token holder proposes an idea to the community.

DELEGATION OF THE VOTE :
The holders of the token of governance can delegate their tokens They fill in the address to which the tokens are sent.
